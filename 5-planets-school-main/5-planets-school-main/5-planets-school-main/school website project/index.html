Kepler-452 b

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kepler-452b
This is one of the most similar planets to earth, It is 60% bigger than earth,"a super earth" as scientists like to call it.
It orbits a star roughly the same size and temoerature as our sun, and it's in the habitable zone. Kepler-452 b has an orbit
of 385 days. It is often referred to as Earth 2.0 or Earths older cousin.
