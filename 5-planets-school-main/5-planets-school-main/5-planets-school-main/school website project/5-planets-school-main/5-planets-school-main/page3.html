Why earth is awesome

Although the rest of the universe is great, let's take a look at why earth is great!

Rich atmosphere


Not only has our atmosphere been through a lot, but even after so much it still continues
to be an amazing source of life, not only does it provide us with nice fresh air, but it
also protects us from deadly awful radation from space and all sorts of nasty things out
there.

Water


Water is great! Not only do they provide us with endless fun, swimming and jumping in it, but
they are also a fundumental element for life on Earth. The water on earth is easy to access as
it is in liquid form, so it's easy to access and drink. Although there might not be much fresh
water on the surface, recently scientists have discovered that there is a lot of water under
earths crust! Maybe even more water than we have on the surface. Better yet, there is also Life
down there!

Life


The thing that earth is most famous for is defenitly is it's abundance of life. It took billions
of years to develop and it's hopefully going to keep developing for billions of years, but life is
of the most magical things on earth, from Amoebas to humans, earth is riddled with complex lifeforms
and it is humans mission to protect all life on earth.
